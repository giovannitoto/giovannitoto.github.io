I am a Postdoctoral fellow at the [Department of Statistics and Data Science](https://stat.utexas.edu/) of the University of Texas at Austin, working with [Yang Ni](https://sites.google.com/view/yangnistat) and [Peter Müller](https://web.ma.utexas.edu/users/pmueller/).
I received my Ph.D. from the [Department of Statistical Sciences](https://www.stat.unipd.it/en/) of the University of Padova, advised by [Antonio Canale](https://tonycanale.github.io/), [Abhra Sarkar](https://abhrastat.github.io/) and [Peter Müller](https://web.ma.utexas.edu/users/pmueller/).

Research interests
------
My research interest are

* Bayesian nonparametrics
* Density estimation
* Functional data analysis
* Random partition models
